Hi, I’m Sindhana, a postdoc in the Limmer Group at UC Berkeley. My research explores how chemical processes are influenced by time-varying media.

For my Ph.D. in Chemistry (2018–2023), I worked with Prof. Joel Yuen-Zhou at UC San Diego, focusing on how strong light-matter coupling affects physical and chemical processes, including radiative heat transfer, condensation, and reactivity. I earned my B.S.-M.S. in Physics (2013–2018) from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, working with Prof. Prabal Maiti.
